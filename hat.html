Instructions

The main view shows a tiling that deforms from tile A at the top to tile B at the bottom.

The upper right view shows tile A in isolation. The lower right view shows tile B.

Click either tile to edit it:

  • a and s to change its side-length ratio

Click the main window to place new tiles (currently disabled):

  • q to rotate left
  • w to rotate right
  • Up/down/left/right to move
  • j to jiggle the piece into alignment with its neighbors
  • ! to seamlessly snap together all displayed corners (or again to unsnap them)
