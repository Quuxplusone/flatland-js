Instructions

The main view shows a tiling that deforms from tile A at the top to tile B at the bottom.

The upper right view shows tile A in isolation. The lower right view shows tile B.

Click either tile to edit it. The heavy black border indicates whether you're editing the upper tile, the lower tile, or both simultaneously.

  • a and s to decrease (increase) its side-length ratio

The following keys have a global effect:

  • + and - to zoom in or zoom out
  • ! to seamlessly snap together all displayed corners (or again to unsnap them)
  • # to display a superimposed triangular grid
  • f to display flipped tiles with a bold outline
  • v to morph top-to-bottom, not left-to-right (or again to undo)
